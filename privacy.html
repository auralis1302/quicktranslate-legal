Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 30, 2026

QuickTranslate ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our mobile application QuickTranslate (the "App").

1. Information We Collect

We collect minimal information necessary to provide our translation services:

  • Text you submit for translation: Text entered for translation is sent to our translation API to process your request. We do not store this text after translation is complete.
  • Camera images: When you use the camera translation feature, images are processed locally on your device and/or sent to our translation service. Images are not stored on our servers.
  • Translation history: Your translation history is stored locally on your device only.
  • Purchase information: We use RevenueCat to manage subscriptions. RevenueCat may collect purchase-related data in accordance with their privacy policy.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information solely to:

  • Provide translation services
  • Process subscription payments
  • Improve the accuracy and performance of the App

3. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • RevenueCat: For subscription management. RevenueCat Privacy Policy
  • Translation API: Text is processed via a third-party translation service to provide translations.

4. Data Storage

Your translation history is stored locally on your device using secure local storage. We do not have access to this data. You can delete this data at any time by uninstalling the App.

5. Children's Privacy

Our App is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the data we hold about you
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Opt out of any data collection

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at: support@quicktranslate.app
