Privacy Policy

QuickTranslate

Last updated: May 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how QuickTranslate collects, uses, and protects information when you use our app.

1. Information We Collect

QuickTranslate does not require you to create an account and does not collect personally identifiable information directly.

  • Translation history is stored locally on your device using an on-device database (Hive). It is never uploaded to our servers.
  • Camera images taken for OCR are processed on-device by Google ML Kit and are not stored or transmitted.
  • Purchase & subscription status is managed by RevenueCat, which assigns an anonymous identifier to your device.

2. Third-Party Services

When you request a translation, the source text is sent to the MyMemory Translation API (api.mymemory.translated.net). Please review MyMemory's own privacy policy for details on how they handle submitted text.

Text-to-speech requests may be forwarded to Lingva (lingva.ml) for cloud-based audio synthesis, or processed locally using your device's built-in TTS engine.

RevenueCat collects anonymous transaction data to manage your subscription. No payment details are ever processed by QuickTranslate itself.

3. Data Retention

All locally stored data (translation history) remains on your device until you delete the app or clear its data. We do not have access to this data.

4. Children's Privacy

QuickTranslate is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by updating the date at the top of this page.
